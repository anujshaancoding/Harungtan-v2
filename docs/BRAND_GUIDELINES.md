## 1. Brand Voice and Tone

### Brand Personality

Harungtan speaks like a stylish, confident friend who knows fashion but never acts pretentious. We are approachable, authentic, and aspirational without being unattainable.

**If Harungtan were a person, they would be:**
- A 25-year-old creative professional who dresses effortlessly well
- Someone who values quality over logos
- Confident but not arrogant, trendy but not a slave to trends
- Proud of being Indian, globally aware

### Voice Characteristics

| Attribute | We Are | We Are Not |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Tone | Confident, warm, relatable | Cocky, cold, corporate |
| Language | Simple, conversational, modern | Jargon-heavy, overly formal, slang-heavy |
| Humor | Witty, clever, self-aware | Sarcastic, crude, offensive |
| Expertise | Knowledgeable, helpful | Preachy, condescending |
| Energy | Energetic, positive, inspiring | Aggressive, desperate, pushy |

### Writing Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use short sentences and paragraphs
- Write like you're talking to a friend
- Use "you" and "your" (speak directly to the customer)
- Be specific: "200 GSM bio-washed cotton" not "premium fabric"
- Show personality: "This tee? Yeah, it's that good."
- Use Indian English naturally: "Rs.", "pincode", "festive season"

**Don't:**
- Use ALL CAPS for emphasis (use bold or italics instead)
- Overuse exclamation marks!!!
- Use corporate speak: "leveraging synergies", "value proposition"
- Be negative about competitors
- Make claims you can't back up
- Use "Dear customer" or "Dear valued patron"

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Product descriptions | Confident, descriptive, aspirational | "Crafted from 200 GSM bio-washed cotton, this oversized tee drapes just right. The kind of tee you'll reach for every morning." |
| Social media | Casual, fun, engaging | "Monday calls for your softest tee. Which color are you reaching for?" |
| Customer support | Warm, empathetic, solution-oriented | "We're sorry about that! Let's get this sorted for you right away. We're sending a replacement today." |
| Email marketing | Friendly, helpful, value-driven | "Your new favorite tee just landed. Here's 10% off because you're part of the family." |
| Error messages | Honest, calm, helpful | "Something went wrong on our end. Please try again, or reach out to us at help@harungtan.com." |
| Sale/promotions | Excited but not desperate | "Our biggest sale of the year is here. Up to 30% off everything. No gimmicks, just great prices." |

### Brand Language

**Words We Use:**
- Premium, quality, crafted, curated
- Comfortable, effortless, everyday
- Made in India, homegrown, local
- Community, family, crew
- Fresh, new drop, just landed
- Thoughtfully made, mindfully designed

**Words We Avoid:**
- Cheap, budget, affordable (use "great value" or "fairly priced")
- Luxury, exclusive (we are accessible, not elitist)
- Fast fashion, trendy (use "modern" or "current")
- Best, #1, world-class (unless substantiated)
- Unisex (use "gender-neutral" or "for everyone")

## 2. Visual Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| Charcoal Black | #1A1A1A | 26, 26, 26 | Primary text, headers, logo |
| Off White | #FAFAF5 | 250, 250, 245 | Backgrounds, negative space |
| Warm Cream | #F5F0E8 | 245, 240, 232 | Secondary backgrounds, cards |

**Accent Colors:**

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-------|-----|-----|-------|
| Terracotta | #C45A3C | 196, 90, 60 | CTAs, highlights, sale tags |
| Olive | #5C6B4F | 92, 107, 79 | Secondary accents, badges |
| Dusty Blue | #6B8BA4 | 107, 139, 164 | Links, subtle accents |

**Neutral Palette:**

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Light Gray | #E8E8E3 | Borders, dividers |
| Medium Gray | #9A9A95 | Secondary text, captions |
| Dark Gray | #4A4A45 | Body text |

**Color Usage Rules:**
- Black and Off White should dominate (80% of any design)
- Terracotta is the primary action color (buttons, CTAs, important callouts)
- Never use more than 3 colors in a single design
- Ensure minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text accessibility

### Typography

**Primary Font: Inter (or similar geometric sans-serif)**
- Available on Google Fonts (free, web-optimized)
- Clean, modern, highly legible at all sizes

| Usage | Font | Weight | Size (Web) | Size (Mobile) |
|-------|------|--------|------------|---------------|
| H1 (Page titles) | Inter | Bold (700) | 36-48px | 28-36px |
| H2 (Section headers) | Inter | Semi-Bold (600) | 28-32px | 22-28px |
| H3 (Subsections) | Inter | Semi-Bold (600) | 22-24px | 18-22px |
| Body text | Inter | Regular (400) | 16px | 14-16px |
| Small text/captions | Inter | Regular (400) | 14px | 12-14px |
| Buttons/CTAs | Inter | Medium (500) | 14-16px | 14px |
| Price display | Inter | Bold (700) | 20-24px | 18-20px |

**Secondary Font (Optional for special use):**
- A serif font like Playfair Display for editorial/blog headings
- Use sparingly for contrast and sophistication

**Typography Rules:**
- Line height: 1.5 for body text, 1.2 for headings
- Letter spacing: normal for body, -0.02em for large headings
- Maximum line width: 700px for body text (readability)
- Always left-align text (never justify on web)

### Photography Style

**Product Photography:**
- Clean white or off-white backgrounds for product pages
- Consistent lighting: soft, even, no harsh shadows
- Show full product with adequate white space
- Multiple angles: front, back, close-up detail, on-model
- Color accuracy is critical (calibrate display for shoots)

**Lifestyle Photography:**
- Natural light preferred (golden hour, window light)
- Urban Indian settings: coffee shops, streets, parks, rooftops
- Diverse models: different body types, skin tones, genders
- Candid feel: models laughing, walking, interacting (not stiff poses)
- Warm color grading: slightly desaturated, warm tones

**Photography Don'ts:**
- No over-filtered or heavily retouched images
- No stock photography for products (always original)
- No cluttered backgrounds that distract from the product
- No unrealistic body standards

**Flat Lay Guidelines:**
- Clean background (white marble, light wood, linen texture)
- Styled with minimal props: sunglasses, coffee cup, plant, book
- Consistent overhead angle
- Products pressed/steamed before shooting

**Image Specifications:**

| Usage | Dimensions | Format | Max Size |
|-------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Product page hero | 1200 x 1600px | WebP/AVIF | 200KB |
| Product thumbnails | 600 x 800px | WebP | 80KB |
| Instagram post | 1080 x 1080px | JPG | 1MB |
| Instagram story | 1080 x 1920px | JPG | 1MB |
| Blog header | 1200 x 630px | WebP | 150KB |
| Email header | 600 x 300px | JPG | 100KB |

## 3. Logo Usage Guidelines

### Logo Versions

The Harungtan logo should be created in the following versions:

1. **Primary Logo:** Full wordmark "HARUNGTAN" in custom typography
2. **Logo + Tagline:** Primary logo with "Wear Your Story" beneath
3. **Icon/Favicon:** Stylized "H" monogram for small applications
4. **Horizontal Layout:** For website header and narrow spaces
5. **Stacked Layout:** For square applications (social media profile, packaging)

### Logo Clear Space
- Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "H" in the wordmark
- No other text, graphics, or elements should intrude into this space

### Logo Color Applications

| Background | Logo Color |
|------------|-----------|
| White/Light backgrounds | Charcoal Black (#1A1A1A) |
| Dark backgrounds | Off White (#FAFAF5) |
| Colored backgrounds | Black or White (whichever has higher contrast) |
| Photography overlays | White with subtle shadow |

### Logo Misuse (Do Not)
- Do not stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
- Do not change the logo colors outside the approved palette
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines, glow)
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Do not recreate the logo in a different font
- Do not make the logo smaller than 80px wide (digital) or 20mm (print)

### Logo File Formats
- **SVG:** For web and digital (scalable)
- **PNG:** For digital with transparency
- **PDF:** For print applications
- **ICO/Favicon:** 32x32px and 16x16px for browser tabs

## 4. Social Media Templates Guidelines

### Instagram Post Templates

**Template 1: Product Showcase**
- Layout: Product image (80% of frame), price badge (bottom-right corner)
- Background: Off White (#FAFAF5)
- Text overlay: Product name in Inter Bold, price in Terracotta
- Footer bar: Brand logo (small, bottom center)

**Template 2: Quote/Text Post**
- Layout: Centered text on solid color background
- Background: Charcoal Black or Terracotta
- Text: Off White, Inter Bold, 2-3 lines maximum
- Brand logo: Bottom corner, small

**Template 3: Carousel (Product Feature)**
- Slide 1: Hero image with product name
- Slide 2-4: Features/details (fabric, fit, styling)
- Slide 5: Price + CTA ("Shop Now at harungtan.com")
- Consistent header bar with brand logo on each slide

**Template 4: Customer Review**
- Layout: Review text centered, star rating above, customer name below
- Background: Warm Cream (#F5F0E8)
- Quote marks in Terracotta
- Product image as small inset (bottom corner)

### Instagram Story Templates

**Template 1: New Arrival**
- Full-bleed product image
- "NEW" badge (Terracotta circle, white text) top-left
- Product name and price as text overlay (bottom)
- "Swipe Up to Shop" or Link sticker

**Template 2: Poll/Engagement**
- Split layout: two product images side by side
- "Which one?" text at top
- Instagram Poll sticker centered

**Template 3: Behind the Scenes**
- Raw, unfiltered photo/video
- "BTS" text label in corner
- Minimal branding (logo watermark only)

### General Social Media Rules

- Maintain consistent visual style across all posts
- Use brand colors for all graphics and overlays
- Never use more than 2 fonts in a single graphic
- Ensure text is legible on mobile (minimum 24pt for Instagram)
- Always include subtle branding (logo or brand colors) on sharable content
- Use consistent filters/presets for photo editing (warm, slightly desaturated)

## 5. Packaging Guidelines

### Unboxing Experience Philosophy

The unboxing experience should feel personal, premium, and environmentally conscious. Every touchpoint is an opportunity to reinforce the brand and encourage sharing on social media.

### Packaging Components

| Component | Specification | Purpose |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| **Outer Mailer** | Black or kraft paper mailer bag (biodegradable) | Protection, first impression |
| **Inner Wrap** | Tissue paper (Off White, with subtle brand pattern) | Reveal moment, premium feel |
| **Thank-You Card** | 4x6 inch card, 300 GSM art paper | Personal touch, repeat purchase incentive |
| **Brand Sticker** | 2-inch circle, Charcoal on Off White | Sealing tissue paper, keepsake |
| **Care Card** | Small card with wash instructions + sizing tips | Reduce returns, add value |
| **Freebie (optional)** | Brand sticker pack or small accessory | Delight, social sharing |

### Outer Mailer Design
- Material: Recyclable poly mailer (if poly) or kraft paper mailer (preferred)
- Color: Matte black or natural kraft brown
- Branding: Logo printed in center (white on black, or black on kraft)
- Size: Fit product with minimal excess space
- Include "Recyclable" and "Made in India" badges

### Thank-You Card

**Front:**
```
Thank you for choosing Harungtan.

You're now part of something special.
This tee was made with care in Tirupur, India.

We hope you love it as much as we loved making it.
```

**Back:**
```
Share your style with us!
Tag @harungtan and use #HarungtanStyle
for a chance to be featured.

Get Rs.100 off your next order: [THANKYOU100]

[QR code linking to review page]
```

- Material: 300 GSM art paper, matte laminated
- Size: 4x6 inches (postcard size)
- Colors: Brand color palette
- Typography: Inter font family

### Brand Labels

**Main Label (Inside neck):**
- Woven label, soft satin
- Content: Harungtan logo + size
- Colors: Black text on white, or white text on black
- Size: 50mm x 20mm

**Care Label (Inside seam):**
- Printed satin
- Content: Fabric composition, wash instructions (icons + text), "Made in India"
- Size: 50mm x 80mm (folded)

**Hang Tag:**
- 300 GSM card stock
- Front: Logo + brand tagline
- Back: Product details, price, QR code to website
- Size: 50mm x 90mm
- Attached with natural cotton string
- Color: Off White with Charcoal text

### Packaging Cost Per Order

| Component | Cost (Rs.) |
|-----------|-----------|
| Poly mailer / kraft bag | Rs.8-15 |
| Tissue paper | Rs.3-5 |
| Thank-you card | Rs.3-5 |
| Brand sticker | Rs.1-2 |
| Care card | Rs.2-3 |
| Hang tag (per product) | Rs.3-5 |
| Woven label (per product) | Rs.4-6 |
| **Total per order** | **Rs.24-41** |

### Sustainability Commitment
- Use biodegradable or recyclable materials wherever possible
- No single-use plastic (no individual poly bags for products)
- Soy-based or water-based inks for printing
- Include a note encouraging customers to reuse/recycle packaging
- Roadmap: move to 100% compostable packaging by Year 2

## 6. Website Design Principles

### Design Philosophy

The Harungtan website should feel like a curated, premium shopping experience. Design decisions prioritize:

1. **Simplicity:** Clean layouts, generous white space, no visual clutter
2. **Speed:** Performance-first approach; every design choice considers load time
3. **Mobile-first:** 70%+ traffic is mobile; design for thumb-friendly interactions
4. **Product-centric:** Products are the hero; design supports, never distracts
5. **Trust:** Clear pricing, visible reviews, transparent policies, secure checkout

### Layout Principles

**Grid System:**
- 12-column grid on desktop
- 4-column grid on mobile
- Consistent gutters: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)
- Maximum content width: 1280px (centered)
- Generous padding: 48-80px between sections (desktop), 32-48px (mobile)

**White Space:**
- White space is a design element, not wasted space
- Minimum 16px spacing between UI elements
- Section padding: never less than 40px vertically
- Product cards: minimum 16px gap between items

### Component Design Standards

**Buttons:**
| Type | Style | Usage |
|------|-------|-------|
| Primary | Solid Charcoal Black, white text | Add to Cart, Checkout, Sign Up |
| Secondary | Outlined, Charcoal border, black text | Add to Wishlist, View Details |
| Accent | Solid Terracotta, white text | Sale CTAs, limited-time offers |
| Ghost | Text only, underline on hover | Links within content |

- Border radius: 6px (slightly rounded, not pill-shaped)
- Minimum size: 44px height (touch-friendly)
- Padding: 12px 24px (standard), 16px 32px (large)
- Hover state: darken background by 10%

**Cards (Product Cards):**
- White background with subtle border or shadow
- Product image: 3:4 aspect ratio
- Hover effect: subtle zoom on image (1.05x scale)
- Content: Product name, price, rating (if available), color swatches
- "New" or "Sale" badges as overlay on image (top-left)

**Forms:**
- Label above input (never placeholder-only labels)
- Input height: 44px minimum
- Clear error messages in red below the field
- Success states with green checkmark
- Autocomplete enabled for address and payment fields

**Navigation:**
- Sticky header on scroll (slim version)
- Mega menu for categories (desktop)
- Bottom navigation bar on mobile (Home, Categories, Cart, Profile)
- Search: prominent, expandable on mobile
- Cart icon with item count badge

### Page-Specific Guidelines

**Homepage:**
- Hero section: full-width lifestyle image or product showcase with CTA
- Category navigation: visual grid with lifestyle images
- Featured/bestseller products: horizontal scroll or grid
- Social proof section: customer reviews, Instagram feed
- Newsletter signup: prominent but not intrusive
- Trust indicators: "Free Shipping over Rs.999", "Easy Returns", "Made in India"

**Product Listing Page (PLP):**
- Filter sidebar (desktop) / bottom sheet (mobile)
- Grid: 3 columns (desktop), 2 columns (mobile)
- Quick-view option on hover (desktop)
- Sort: Newest, Price Low-High, Price High-Low, Rating, Bestselling
- Infinite scroll or "Load More" (not traditional pagination for better UX)

**Product Detail Page (PDP):**
- Image gallery: large hero image + thumbnails (desktop), swipeable carousel (mobile)
- Sticky "Add to Cart" bar on mobile (visible when scrolling)
- Size selector with "Size Guide" link
- Color selector with visual swatches
- Accordion sections: Description, Material & Care, Shipping, Reviews
- "You May Also Like" product recommendations
- Social sharing buttons

**Cart Page:**
- Clear item list with images, name, size, color, quantity, price
- Quantity selector (+/- buttons)
- Remove button
- Order summary sidebar with subtotal, shipping, total
- "Continue Shopping" and "Proceed to Checkout" CTAs
- Free shipping progress bar: "Add Rs.X more for free shipping!"

**Checkout Page:**
- Minimal distractions (no header navigation, no footer links)
- Step indicator: Shipping > Payment > Confirmation
- Address form with pincode auto-fill for city/state
- Order summary always visible
- Trust badges: "Secure Payment", "SSL Encrypted", Stripe logo
- Multiple payment options clearly displayed

### Accessibility Standards

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance target
- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- All images have descriptive alt text
- Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
- Focus indicators visible on all focusable elements
- Screen reader compatible form labels and error messages
- Touch targets: minimum 44x44px

### Performance Standards

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) | < 2.5 seconds |
| First Input Delay (FID) | < 100ms |
| Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) | < 0.1 |
| Time to Interactive (TTI) | < 3.5 seconds |
| Total page weight (initial load) | < 1MB |
| Image format | WebP/AVIF with fallbacks |
