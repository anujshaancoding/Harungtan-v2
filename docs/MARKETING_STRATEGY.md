## 1. Social Media Strategy

### Platform Priority

| Platform | Priority | Purpose | Content Frequency |
|----------|----------|---------|-------------------|
| Instagram | Primary (70% effort) | Discovery, engagement, sales | 4-5 posts + 7-10 stories/week |
| Facebook | Secondary (10%) | Ads platform, older demographic | 2-3 posts/week (cross-post) |
| YouTube | Growth (10%) | Long-form content, brand storytelling | 1-2 videos/month |
| Pinterest | Discovery (5%) | Traffic, fashion inspiration | 10-15 pins/week |
| Twitter/X | Community (5%) | Brand personality, customer service | 3-5 tweets/week |

### Instagram Strategy

**Account Setup:**
- Username: @harungtan
- Bio: "Premium T-shirts | Made in India | Free Shipping 999+" + link to website
- Highlights: New Arrivals, Bestsellers, Reviews, Size Guide, Behind the Scenes, Sale
- Contact: WhatsApp button + email

**Content Pillars (Rule of Thirds):**

1. **Product Content (40%)**
   - Product flat lays with clean backgrounds
   - On-model product shots (diverse body types)
   - Close-up fabric/detail shots
   - New arrival announcements
   - Restock alerts

2. **Lifestyle Content (30%)**
   - Outfit inspiration / styling ideas
   - "How to style" Reels (3 ways to wear a basic tee)
   - Street style photography
   - Day-to-night outfit transitions
   - Seasonal styling content

3. **Brand/Community Content (30%)**
   - Behind-the-scenes (packing orders, visiting manufacturers, design process)
   - Customer spotlights and UGC reposts
   - Team/founder stories
   - Polls, Q&As, "This or That" Stories
   - Memes and relatable fashion content
   - Sustainability initiatives

**Instagram Reels Strategy:**
- Post 3-5 Reels/week (highest organic reach format)
- Target length: 15-30 seconds for trends, 30-60 seconds for tutorials
- Trending audio usage within 48 hours of trending
- Hooks in first 1-2 seconds: "Stop scrolling if you love oversized tees"
- Content ideas:
  - "GRWM with Harungtan"
  - "Packing your order" ASMR-style
  - "Rs.699 vs Rs.2,999 t-shirt comparison"
  - "3 ways to style our bestseller"
  - Behind-the-scenes factory visits
  - Customer review reading reactions

**Instagram Stories Strategy:**
- 7-10 Stories daily
- Mix: Product launches (2), BTS (2), Polls/engagement (2), UGC/reviews (2), Personal/fun (1-2)
- Use Link stickers to drive traffic to website
- "Add Yours" stickers for UGC
- Countdown stickers for launches and sales

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Brand hashtags: #Harungtan #WearYourStory #HarungtanStyle
- Category hashtags: #OversizedTshirts #GraphicTees #MadeInIndia
- Community hashtags: #IndianFashion #StreetStyleIndia #OOTD
- Use 15-20 hashtags per post (mix of low, medium, high competition)

### Facebook Strategy
- Cross-post Instagram content
- Create a Facebook Shop (linked to website catalog)
- Run a Facebook Group: "Harungtan Community" for exclusive drops and feedback
- Primary value: advertising platform (Meta Ads Manager)

### YouTube Strategy (Month 4+)
- Channel name: Harungtan
- Content types:
  - Lookbooks (seasonal, 3-5 min)
  - "How It's Made" -- manufacturing process (5-8 min)
  - Haul videos with customer reviews
  - Brand story / founder journey
  - Fashion tips and trends
- YouTube Shorts: Repurpose Instagram Reels

### Pinterest Strategy (Month 5+)
- Create boards: Men's T-shirts, Women's T-shirts, Street Style, Outfit Ideas, Color Palettes
- Pin every product with optimized descriptions (keywords)
- Create style guide infographics
- Link every pin to product page
- Pinterest drives high-intent traffic (users actively looking for fashion ideas)

## 3. Influencer Marketing

### Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Engagement Rate | Cost | Purpose |
|------|-----------|----------------|------|---------|
| Nano | 1K-5K | 5-8% | Free product | UGC content, authentic reviews |
| Micro | 5K-25K | 3-6% | Free product + Rs.500-2,000 | Reach, social proof |
| Mid | 25K-100K | 2-4% | Rs.3,000-10,000 | Brand awareness, conversions |
| Macro | 100K-500K | 1.5-3% | Rs.15,000-50,000 | Major awareness (rare, strategic) |

### Month-by-Month Influencer Plan

**Month 3-4 (Budget: Rs.5,000-15,000)**
- 5-8 nano/micro influencers (product gifting only)
- Focus: Instagram fashion bloggers in metro cities
- Deliverables: 1 Reel + 2 Stories per influencer
- Track with unique discount codes (e.g., PRIYA10, ARJUN15)

**Month 5-6 (Budget: Rs.15,000-30,000)**
- 10-15 nano/micro influencers (product gifting)
- 2-3 mid-tier influencers (paid: Rs.5,000-10,000 each)
- Focus: Fashion, lifestyle, college student influencers
- Launch #HarungtanStyle campaign -- encourage followers to style and tag

**Month 7-8 (Budget: Rs.25,000-50,000)**
- Continue micro-influencer pipeline (10+ per month)
- 3-5 mid-tier influencers (paid collaborations)
- Explore YouTube fashion influencers for haul videos
- Consider 1 macro influencer collaboration (if budget allows)

**Month 9-12 (Budget: Rs.40,000-80,000/month)**
- Establish long-term ambassador program (3-5 recurring influencers)
- Influencer capsule collection (co-designed with a popular creator)
- Festival special collaborations
- Affiliate program for influencers (10-15% commission on sales)

## 6. Paid Advertising

### Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook)

**Campaign Structure:**

```
Campaign Level:
├── Prospecting (60% budget) -- Finding new customers
│   ├── Interest-based targeting
│   │   ├── Fashion enthusiasts, 18-28, metro cities
│   │   ├── Competitor audiences (Bewakoof, Souled Store fans)
│   │   └── Lifestyle interests (streetwear, Indian fashion)
│   ├── Lookalike audiences
│   │   ├── 1% lookalike of purchasers
│   │   ├── 1% lookalike of add-to-cart
│   │   └── 2% lookalike of website visitors
│   └── Broad targeting (25+, India, male/female)
│
├── Retargeting (30% budget) -- Converting warm audiences
│   ├── Website visitors (last 30 days) -- exclude purchasers
│   ├── Instagram engagers (last 60 days)
│   ├── Add-to-cart but not purchased (last 14 days)
│   └── Video viewers (75%+ watched)
│
└── Retention (10% budget) -- Repeat purchases
    ├── Past customers (30-90 days ago)
    └── Email list custom audiences
```

**Ad Creative Best Practices:**
- Use UGC-style content (performs 2-3x better than polished ads)
- Video ads: 15-30 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds
- Carousel ads: Show product + lifestyle + review + offer
- Always show price prominently for fashion ads
- Test 3-5 creatives per ad set, kill underperformers after 3-5 days
- Winning formula: "Problem > Solution > Social Proof > CTA"

**Monthly Budget Scaling:**

|-------|-------------|----------------|-------|

| M4 | Rs.500-1,000 | Rs.15K-30K | Scale winning ads |
| M5-6 | Rs.1,000-2,000 | Rs.30K-60K | Aggressive prospecting |
| M7-8 | Rs.1,500-2,500 | Rs.45K-75K | Optimize ROAS |

## 7. Referral Program

### Program Design: "Share the Love"

**Mechanics:**
- Existing customer shares a unique referral link
- New customer gets Rs.100 off their first order (minimum order Rs.599)
- Referring customer gets Rs.100 store credit when the referee completes their first purchase
- Store credit can be used on next purchase (no expiry, minimum order Rs.499)

**Implementation:**
- Referral link format: harungtan.com/?ref=CUSTOMER_CODE
- Auto-generate unique code for each customer after first purchase
- Display referral dashboard in customer profile
- Send automated email post-purchase with referral link

**Promotion:**
- Post-purchase email: "Love your tee? Share Rs.100 with a friend"
- In-package insert card: QR code to referral page
- Website: Dedicated referral page accessible from footer and profile
- Social media: Monthly "refer and win" campaigns

**Expected Metrics:**
- Referral rate: 5-10% of customers refer at least 1 friend
- Conversion rate of referred traffic: 15-25% (higher than cold traffic)
- CAC via referral: Rs.100 (vs. Rs.150-250 via paid ads)

## 8. Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (2-3 Weeks Before)

**Week 1: Build Anticipation**
- [ ] Start posting teaser content on Instagram (blurred products, fabric close-ups)
- [ ] "Something special is coming" Stories with countdown
- [ ] Share brand story: why Harungtan exists, what makes it different
- [ ] Collect emails via "Get Early Access" landing page

**Week 2: Generate Buzz**
- [ ] Reveal brand name, logo, and visual identity
- [ ] Share product teasers: one product reveal per day
- [ ] "Behind the Making" content: factory, fabric, quality checks
- [ ] Influencer seeding: send products to 5-8 micro-influencers (embargo until launch day)
- [ ] Instagram countdown sticker: "Launching in X days"

**Week 3: Final Push**
- [ ] Full product catalog preview (carousel posts)
- [ ] Launch date announcement
- [ ] Early access signup: "Be the first 50 to order, get 20% off"
- [ ] WhatsApp broadcast to personal network
- [ ] Email blast to pre-launch list

### Launch Day

**Morning:**
- [ ] Website goes live (double-check all product pages, checkout flow, payment)
- [ ] Instagram announcement post + Reel (launch video)
- [ ] Instagram Stories: swipe-up links to products
- [ ] Email blast: "We're Live! Shop Now + Get 20% Off"
- [ ] WhatsApp broadcast to all contacts

**Afternoon:**
- [ ] Instagram Live: product showcase, Q&A, live sizing demo
- [ ] Respond to all DMs and comments within 1 hour
- [ ] Monitor website for any issues (payment, loading, errors)
- [ ] Influencer posts go live (coordinated timing)

**Evening:**
- [ ] Share first-day sales milestone Stories (if impressive)
- [ ] Thank you post for early supporters
- [ ] Repost customer/influencer content

### Post-Launch (Week 1-2)

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Maintain daily posting cadence
- [ ] Send "Thank You" email to all first-day buyers
- [ ] Address any quality/shipping concerns immediately
- [ ] Run "Launch Week" promotion: free shipping on all orders
- [ ] Collect and share first customer reviews/photos

**Week 2:**
- [ ] Launch first paid ad campaign (Meta Ads) using launch content
- [ ] Create retargeting audience from launch week visitors
- [ ] Analyze launch data: which products sold, demographics, traffic sources
- [ ] Plan restocking of fast-selling items
- [ ] Post "Behind the Scenes: Our First Week" content
